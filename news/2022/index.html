Summary of 2022

By Manika Lamba in news

January 1, 2022

  • Received $5000 (US) from Princeton University under the Data Driven Social Science (DDSS) initiative

  • The dataset for the article, “Gender in the Journals: A Dataset of Gender and Politics Research” is now available at https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/UVWRTV

  • Received funding to make the accepted article, “Gender in the Journals: A Dataset of Gender and Politics Research” in open access by Princeton University

  • The manuscript entitled, “Gender in the Journals: A Dataset of Gender and Politics Research” got accepted in PS: Political Science & Politics journal published by Cambridge University Press.

  • The edited book, “Technological Advancements in Library Service Innovation” is now available on IGI Global and Amazon

  • Invited to be a jury on the JASIST Best Paper Award

  • Volunteered to serve on the for Program and Schedule subcommittee for CarpentryCon

  • Appointed on the Student Board for Association for Information Science & Technology (ASIS&T) Digital Forum - Information Matters. I will be working with Multimedia production Team which involves producing and coordinate the production of videos (interviews, tutorials, viewpoints), and podcasts
